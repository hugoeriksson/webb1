Voith Turbo SafeSet Products

Voith Turbo SafeSet specializes in advanced torque-limiting and connection couplings designed to protect drivetrains from overload, ensuring reliability and efficiency in various industries. These products are essential in applications where precise torque transmission and safety are critical, such as mining, marine, energy, and heavy machinery.

Key SafeSet Products

SafeSet Torque Limiters

  • Automatically disengage during overload to prevent damage.
  • Resettable design for minimal downtime.
  • Precise torque settings to protect equipment.

SlipSet

  • Allows controlled slipping to absorb shock loads.
  • Ideal for applications with frequent load changes.

SmartSet

  • Adaptive torque-limiting coupling with monitoring capabilities.
  • Enables real-time adjustments to optimize performance.

AutoSet

  • Self-adjusting coupling for automated load management.
  • Improves efficiency in high-demand applications.

These solutions enhance equipment lifespan, reduce maintenance costs, and improve overall system performance. With a focus on safety and innovation, Voith Turbo SafeSet ensures maximum protection for critical industrial applications.
