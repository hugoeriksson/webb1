Voith today: A Global Technology Leader

Company overview

Voith is a German multinational company specializing in engineering and industrial solutions. Founded in 1867, it operates in various sectors, including energy, paper, raw materials, and transportation. With a strong focus on innovation and sustainability, Voith continues to be a key player in advancing industrial technologies.

Key Business Areas

Voith operates through three main divisions:Voith Hydro: Provides solutions for hydropower generation, including turbines and automation systems.Voith Paper: Develops technologies for paper production, focusing on efficiency and sustainability. Voith Turbo: Specializes in drive and automation solutions for transportation, mining, and industrial applications.

Commitment to Sustainability

Voith integrates sustainability into its operations by developing energy-efficient products and promoting circular economy principles. The company actively invests in digitalization and environmentally friendly technologies to reduce carbon emissions and enhance resource efficiency.
