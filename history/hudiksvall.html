Voith Turbo Safeset AB in Hudiksvall: Pioneering Torque Limiting Solutions

Establishment and Expertise

Voith Turbo Safeset AB, located in Hudiksvall, Sweden, specializes in developing and manufacturing advanced torque limiting couplings, process improvers, and connection couplings. Established in 1982, the company has over 30 years of experience in protecting drivelines in rotating equipment across various industries, including mining, steel, marine, energy, rail, and recycling. With a strong focus on innovation and quality, Voith Turbo Safeset AB provides reliable, high-performance solutions that ensure operational efficiency, reduce downtime, and extend the lifespan of critical machinery in harsh industrial environments worldwide.

Innovative Product Range

The company's product portfolio includes: Torque Limiting Couplings: These couplings protect machinery from overload by releasing or slipping at a precise set torque, thereby maximizing production uptime. Connection Couplings: Hydraulically set and reset couplings, such as HyCon, HyLoc, and HyGrip, provide high-performance shaft connections, ensuring reliability and efficiency in various applications.

Commitment to Research and Development

Continuous research and development form the backbone of Voith Turbo Safeset AB's product innovation. The company's technical department designs new drivelines or retrofits existing ones, simulating various design proposals to maximize potential without risking damage. This dedication ensures that their solutions meet the evolving demands of modern driveline technology. Through its expertise and commitment to innovation, Voith Turbo Safeset AB in Hudiksvall continues to lead in providing advanced solutions for driveline protection and optimization.
